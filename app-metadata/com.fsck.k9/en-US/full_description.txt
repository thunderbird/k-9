Open source e-mail client that works with basically every e-mail provider.

<b>Features</b>

* Multiple accounts
* Unified Inbox
* Privacy-friendly (no tracking whatsoever, only connects to your e-mail provider)
* Automatic background synchronization or push notifications
* Local and server-side search
* OpenPGP e-mail encryption (PGP/MIME)

Install the app <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/org.sufficientlysecure.keychain">OpenKeychain: Easy PGP</a> to encrypt/decrypt your e-mails using OpenPGP.
